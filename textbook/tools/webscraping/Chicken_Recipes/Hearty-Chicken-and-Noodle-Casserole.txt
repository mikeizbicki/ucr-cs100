--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Hearty Chicken and Noodle Casserole
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
1 (10.75 ounce) can
Campbell's&#174; Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup (Regular, 98% Fat Free or 25% Less Sodium)
1/2 cup
milk
1/4 teaspoon
ground black pepper
1 cup
frozen mixed vegetables
2 cups
cubed, cooked chicken
2 cups
medium egg noodles, cooked and drained
1/4 cup
grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup
shredded Cheddar cheese
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Stir soup, milk, black pepper, vegetables, chicken, noodles and Parmesan cheese in 1 1/2-quart casserole
Bake at 400 degrees F for 25 minutes or until hot. Stir
Top with the Cheddar cheese
--------------------------------------------------
