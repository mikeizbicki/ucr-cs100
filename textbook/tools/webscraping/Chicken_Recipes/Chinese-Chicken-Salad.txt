--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Chinese Chicken Salad
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
3 1/2
boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and diced
1 head
lettuce, torn into small pieces
4
green onions, sliced
4 stalks
celery, sliced thin
1/2 cup
walnuts, chopped
2 tablespoons
sesame seeds, toasted
6 ounces
Chinese noodles, heated briefly to crisp
6 tablespoons
seasoned rice vinegar
4 tablespoons
white sugar
1 teaspoon
salt
1/2 cup
peanut oil
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
In a large salad bowl combine the chicken, lettuce, green onion, celery, nuts, seeds and noodles. Mix all together. Set aside
To Make Dressing: Put vinegar in a small bowl. Dissolve sugar and salt in vinegar before adding oil. Shake/Beat well
