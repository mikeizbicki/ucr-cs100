--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Angel Hair Pasta with Lemon and Chicken (Lighter)
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
1 (9 ounce) package
BUITONI&#174; Refrigerated Angel Hair Pasta
1 1/2 cups
diced cooked chicken
2 tablespoons
extra virgin olive oil
1 tablespoon
butter, melted
2 tablespoons
lemon juice, or more to taste
2 tablespoons
chopped fresh parsley
1/4 teaspoon
dried marjoram
1/4 teaspoon
garlic powder
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Prepare pasta according to package directions
Toss pasta with chicken, oil, butter, lemon juice, parsley, marjoram and garlic powder. Season with salt and ground black pepper, if desired
