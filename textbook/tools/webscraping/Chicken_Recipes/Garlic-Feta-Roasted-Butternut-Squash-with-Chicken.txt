--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Garlic-Feta Roasted Butternut Squash with Chicken
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
2 pounds
cubed butternut squash
2 large
skinless, boneless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
1 large
sweet red onion, or to taste, finely chopped
1 cup
Mediterranean-style (with basil, Kalamata olive, and sun-dried tomato) crumbled feta cheese
2 tablespoons
extra-virgin olive oil
2 cloves
garlic, or to taste, minced
1 pinch
ground multi-colored peppercorns, or to taste
1 pinch
sea salt, or to taste
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
Mix butternut squash, chicken, red onion, feta cheese, olive oil, garlic, pepper, and sea salt together in a roasting pan
Roast in preheated oven, stirring every ten minutes or so, until the chicken is no longer pink in the center and the squash cubes are tender, 30 to 40 minutes
