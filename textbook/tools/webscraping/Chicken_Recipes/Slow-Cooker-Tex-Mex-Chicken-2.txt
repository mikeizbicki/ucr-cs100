--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Slow-Cooker Tex-Mex Chicken
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
1 pound
boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch-wide strips
2 tablespoons
taco seasoning mix
2 tablespoons
flour
1
green pepper, cut into 1-inch-wide strips
1
red pepper, cut into 1-inch-wide strips
1 cup
frozen corn
1 1/2 cups
chunky salsa
1 cup
KRAFT Tex Mex Shredded Cheese
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Toss chicken with seasoning and flour in slow cooker. Stir in all remaining ingredients except shredded cheese; cover with lid
Cook on LOW 6 to 8 hours (or on HIGH 3 to 4 hours)
Stir before serving. Top with shredded cheese
--------------------------------------------------
