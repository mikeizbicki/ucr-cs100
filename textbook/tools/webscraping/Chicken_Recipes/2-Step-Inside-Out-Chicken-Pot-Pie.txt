--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
2-Step Inside-Out Chicken Pot Pie
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
1 pound
skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut-up
1 (10.75 ounce) can
Campbell's&#174; Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup (Regular or 98% Fat Free)
1 (16 ounce) package
frozen vegetable combination (broccoli, cauliflower, carrots)
8
hot biscuits, split
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Cook chicken in nonstick skillet until browned, stirring often
Add soup and vegetables. Cover and simmer until done. Serve on biscuits
