--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Maille&#174; Honey Dijon Chicken
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
1 (3 pound)
whole chicken
3 tablespoons
Maille&#174; Honey Dijon Mustard
2 tablespoons
cider vinegar
Juice of half a lemon
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Coat whole chicken with a mixture of Maille&#174; Honey Dijon, cider vinegar and lemon juice
Season chicken with salt and pepper and arrange in an ovenproof casserole
Brown the chicken, then cover with a lid and let cook for another 45 minutes or until thoroughly cooked. Serve with seasonal vegetables cooked in butter
--------------------------------------------------
