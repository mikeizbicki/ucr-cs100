--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Easy Chinese Chicken Salad
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
1
rotisserie chicken, meat removed and shredded
1/2 medium head
cabbage, chopped
4
green onions, sliced
1 (3 ounce) package
ramen noodles (such as Nissin&#174; Top Ramen)
3 tablespoons
sesame seeds
3 tablespoons
slivered almonds
1/2 cup
olive oil
3 tablespoons
apple cider vinegar
2 tablespoons
white sugar
1 teaspoon
salt
1 teaspoon
ground black pepper
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Toss chicken meat, cabbage, onions, ramen noodles, sesame seeds, and almonds together in a large bowl. Beat olive oil, vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper together in a small bowl; drizzle over the salad and toss to coat
