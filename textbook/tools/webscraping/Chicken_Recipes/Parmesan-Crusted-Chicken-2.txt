--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Parmesan Crusted Chicken
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
1/2 cup
Hellmann's&#174; or Best Foods&#174; Real Mayonnaise
1/4 cup
grated Parmesan cheese
4 (5 ounce)
boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
4 teaspoons
Italian seasoned dry bread crumbs
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
Combine Hellmann's&#174; or Best Foods&#174; Real Mayonnaise with cheese in medium bowl
Arrange chicken on baking sheet. Evenly top with mayonnaise mixture, then sprinkle with bread crumbs
Bake 20 minutes or until chicken is thoroughly cooked
