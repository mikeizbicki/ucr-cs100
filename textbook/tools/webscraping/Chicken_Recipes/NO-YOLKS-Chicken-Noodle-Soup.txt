--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
NO YOLKS&#174; Chicken Noodle Soup
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
6 ounces
NO YOLKS&#174; Extra Broad Noodles
1 tablespoon
vegetable oil
2 cloves
garlic, chopped
1
onion, chopped
2
carrots, peeled and sliced
2 ribs
celery, sliced
2
bay leaves
2 sprigs
fresh thyme
1
rotisserie chicken, shredded, skin and bones removed*
4 quarts
chicken broth
Salt and pepper
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add garlic and onions and saute 2 minutes
Add carrots, celery, bay leaves, thyme, chicken and broth. Bring to a boil. Add noodles. Reduce heat to medium and simmer 10 minutes. Remove bay leaves and thyme sprigs. Season with salt and pepper to taste
