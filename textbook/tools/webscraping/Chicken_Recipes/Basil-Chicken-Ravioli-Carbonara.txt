--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Basil Chicken Ravioli Carbonara
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
1 (9 ounce) package
refrigerated four-cheese-filled ravioli
1 (14 ounce) package
uncooked chicken breast pieces for stir-fry
1 (9 ounce) pouch
Progresso&#8482; Recipe Starters&#8482; creamy Parmesan basil cooking sauce
1/4 cup
half-and-half
1/4 cup
cooked real bacon pieces (from a jar or package)
Shredded Asiago or Parmesan cheese
Chopped fresh basil leaves or parsley
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Cook and drain ravioli as directed on package
In 10-inch skillet, heat 1 tablespoon vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add chicken; cook 2 to 4 minutes, turning occasionally, until brown. Stir in cooking sauce, cooked ravioli and half-and-half; reduce heat. Simmer uncovered 3 to 5 minutes or until sauce is hot
Sprinkle with bacon, cheese and basil
