--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Chicken Claridge Stew
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
3
skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into cubes
1
onion, chopped
2 stalks
celery, chopped
4
carrots, chopped
1 (10.75 ounce) can
condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can
condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 cup
chicken broth
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 teaspoon
dried sage
1 clove
garlic, minced
1 (14.5 ounce) can
diced tomatoes
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Spray a large skillet with cooking spray. Saute the chicken in the oil. Add the onion and saute until translucent. Add the celery and carrots and saute briefly
