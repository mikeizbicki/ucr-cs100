--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Easy Mac and Cheese Veggie Chicken Casserole from Country Crock&#174;
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
4 tablespoons
Country Crock&#174; Spread, melted, divided
1 cup
2% milk
8 ounces
dry rotini pasta, cooked and drained
2 cups
diced cooked chicken
4 cups
assorted frozen vegetables, thawed
2 1/4 cups
finely shredded low-fat Cheddar cheese
1/4 cup
plain dry bread crumbs
1 tablespoon
grated Parmesan cheese
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Combine 3 tablespoons Country Crock&#174; Spread, milk, rotini, chicken, vegetables and cheddar cheese in 8-inch baking dish
Combine bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese and remaining Spread in small bowl; sprinkle over top of casserole
Bake until heated through and crumbs are toasted, about 30 minutes
