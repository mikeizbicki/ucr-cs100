Name
--------------------------------------------------
Easy Italian Chicken
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
8
chicken legs, halved
4 cloves
garlic
1 tablespoon
crushed red pepper flakes
1 tablespoon
vegetable oil
salt to taste
1/2 cup
water
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
In a large skillet, brown the chicken pieces in oil and cook for about 15 minutes over medium heat
