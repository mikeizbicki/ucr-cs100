--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Campbell's Kitchen Easy Chicken Pot Pie
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
1 (10.75 ounce) can
Campbell's&#174; Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup (regular, 98% Fat Free, or 25% Less Sodium)*
1 (10 ounce) package
frozen mixed vegetables, thawed
1 cup
cubed cooked chicken or turkey
1/2 cup
milk
1
egg
1 cup
all-purpose baking mix
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
Mix soup, vegetables and chicken in 9-inch pie plate
Mix milk, egg and baking mix. Pour over chicken mixture. Bake 30 minutes or until golden
