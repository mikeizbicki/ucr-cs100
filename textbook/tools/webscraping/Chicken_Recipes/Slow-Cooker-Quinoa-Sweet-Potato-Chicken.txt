--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Slow Cooker Quinoa Sweet Potato Chicken
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
2 (14.5 ounce) cans
chicken broth
2 1/2 cups
chopped cooked chicken, or more to taste
1 (28 ounce) can
petite-cut diced tomatoes with garlic and olive oil
2 (16 ounce) cans
red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 (15 ounce) can
black beans, drained and rinsed
2 large
sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into cubes
1/2 cup
quinoa
1 tablespoon
dried minced onion
1 tablespoon
chili powder
1 teaspoon
minced garlic
1/4 teaspoon
red pepper flakes
salt and ground black pepper to taste
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Stir chicken broth, chopped chicken, tomatoes, kidney beans, black beans, sweet potatoes, quinoa, minced onion, chili powder, minced garlic, red pepper flakes, salt, and black pepper together in a slow cooker
Cook in the slow cooker set to Low for 8 hours
