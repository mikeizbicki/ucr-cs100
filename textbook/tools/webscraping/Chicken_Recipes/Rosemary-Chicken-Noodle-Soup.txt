--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Rosemary Chicken Noodle Soup
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
8 cups
water
2 pounds
boneless, skinless chicken meat
2 tablespoons
vegetable oil
2 tablespoons
dried rosemary
1 tablespoon
dried thyme
3 cloves
crushed garlic
1
white onion
4
carrots, sliced
1 (16 ounce) package
penne pasta
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
In a large kettle, boil water, chicken, spices, onion, and garlic for 1 hour
Add carrots, oil, and noodles; boil for 20 minutes. Salt and pepper to taste
