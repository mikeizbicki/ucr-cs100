--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Slow Cooker Hawaiian Chicken
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
3 pounds
chicken breast
1 (18 ounce) bottle
honey barbecue sauce
1 (15.25 ounce) can
sliced pineapple
1 teaspoon
soy sauce
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Put chicken breasts into the crock of a slow cooker; top with barbeque sauce, pineapple slices, and soy sauce
Cook on High for 4 to 5 hours
--------------------------------------------------
