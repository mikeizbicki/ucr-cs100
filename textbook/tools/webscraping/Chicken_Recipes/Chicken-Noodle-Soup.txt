--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Chicken Noodle Soup
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
4 cups
chopped, cooked chicken meat
1 cup
chopped celery
1/4 cup
chopped carrots
1/4 cup
chopped onion
1/4 cup
butter
8 ounces
egg noodles
12 cups
water
9 cubes
chicken bouillon
1/2 teaspoon
dried marjoram
1/2 teaspoon
ground black pepper
1
bay leaf
1 tablespoon
dried parsley
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
In a large stock pot, saute celery and onion in butter or margarine
Add chicken, carrots, water, bouillon cubes, marjoram, black pepper, bay leaf, and parsley. Simmer for 30 minutes
Add noodles, and simmer for 10 more minutes
