--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Quick Chicken Paella
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
1 tablespoon
vegetable oil
1 pound
boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut in bite-sized pieces
2 links
chorizo sausage, thinly sliced
1 (14 ounce) bag
Birds Eye&#174; Recipe Ready Mixed Vegetables
1 (1 ounce) package
taco seasoning blend
1 (10 ounce) bag
Birds Eye&#174; Steamfresh&#174; Long Grain White Rice, cooked according to package directions
1/2 cup
chicken broth
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Heat oil in large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat and cook chicken and chorizo, stirring frequently, 8 minutes or until chicken is almost done

Stir in prepared rice and chicken broth; heat through. Season, if desired, with salt and pepper
