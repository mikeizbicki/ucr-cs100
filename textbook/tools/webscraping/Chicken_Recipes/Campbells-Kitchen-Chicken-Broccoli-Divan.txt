--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Campbell's Kitchen Chicken Broccoli Divan
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
1 pound
fresh broccoli, cut into spears
1 1/2 cups
cubed cooked chicken or turkey
1 (10.75 ounce) can
Campbell's&#174; Condensed Broccoli Cheese Soup or Campbell's&#174; Condensed 98% Fat Free Broccoli Cheese Soup*
1/3 cup
milk
1/2 cup
shredded Cheddar cheese
2 tablespoons
dry bread crumbs
1 tablespoon
butter or margarine, melted
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Arrange broccoli and chicken in 9-inch pie plate or 2-quart shallow baking dish
Mix soup and milk and pour over all
Sprinkle with cheese. Mix bread crumbs with butter and sprinkle on top
Bake 400 degrees F for 25 minutes or until hot
