--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Mexican Chicken and Rice Salad
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
3 cups
cooked brown rice
2 cups
salsa
1 (15.25 ounce) can
sweet corn, drained
1 (15 ounce) can
black beans, rinsed and drained
1
cooked chicken breast, chopped
1
avocado - peeled, pitted, and cut into cubes
1/4 cup
chopped cilantro
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Mix rice, salsa, corn, beans, chicken, avocado, and cilantro together in a bowl
--------------------------------------------------
