--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Easy Chicken and Biscuits
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
1 (10.75 ounce) can
Campbell's&#174; Condensed Cream of Celery Soup or Campbell's&#174; Condensed 98% Fat Free Cream of Celery Soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can
Campbell's&#174; Condensed Cream of Potato Soup
1 cup
milk
1/4 teaspoon
dried thyme leaves, crushed
1/4 teaspoon
ground black pepper
4 cups
cooked cut-up vegetables*
2 cups
cubed cooked chicken
1 (7 ounce) can
refrigerated buttermilk biscuits
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Mix soups, milk, thyme, black pepper, vegetables and chicken in 3-quart shallow baking dish
Bake at 400 degrees F for 15 minutes. Stir
Cut each biscuit into quarters
Arrange cut biscuits over chicken mixture. Bake 15 minutes or until biscuits are golden
