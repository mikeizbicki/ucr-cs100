--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Slow Cooker Spaghetti Squash Chicken Soup
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
12 ounces
spaghetti squash, peeled, seeded, and cut into 3-inch pieces
1 (14.5 ounce) can
petite diced tomatoes
1 cup
chicken broth
8 ounces
chicken breast, diced
1/2
green bell pepper, chopped
1 teaspoon
salt
1 teaspoon
white sugar
1 teaspoon
chopped fresh parsley
1/2 teaspoon
basil
1/4 teaspoon
oregano leaves
1/4 teaspoon
garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon
onion powder
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Stir spaghetti squash, tomatoes, chicken broth, chicken, bell pepper, salt, sugar, parsley, basil, oregano, garlic powder, and onion powder together in a slow cooker
Cook on Low for 5 to 6 hours
--------------------------------------------------
