--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Slow Cooker Cranberry Chicken
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
4
skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 (16 ounce) bottle
Catalina salad dressing
1 (14.5 ounce) can
whole berry cranberry sauce
1 envelope
onion soup mix
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Place the chicken breasts in the bottom of a slow cooker. Pour the salad dressing, cranberry sauce, and onion soup mix over the chicken
Cook on Low 4 to 6 hours
--------------------------------------------------
