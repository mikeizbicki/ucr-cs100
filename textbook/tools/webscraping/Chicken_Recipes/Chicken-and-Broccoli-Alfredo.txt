--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Chicken and Broccoli Alfredo
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
1/2 (16 ounce) package
linguine
1 cup
fresh or frozen broccoli flowerets
2 tablespoons
butter
1 pound
skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut into cubes
1 (10.75 ounce) can
Campbell's&#174; Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup (Regular, 98% Fat Free or 25% Less Sodium)
1/2 cup
milk
1/2 cup
grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 teaspoon
ground black pepper
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Cook linguine according to package directions. Add broccoli for last 4 minutes of cooking time. Drain
Heat butter in skillet. Cook chicken until browned, stirring often
Add soup, milk, cheese, black pepper and linguine mixture and heat through. Serve with additional Parmesan cheese
