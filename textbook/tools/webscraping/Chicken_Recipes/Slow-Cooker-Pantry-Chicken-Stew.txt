--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Slow-Cooker Pantry Chicken Stew
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
1 1/2 pounds
boneless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces
1/4 cup
flour
1 (8 ounce) package
sliced fresh mushrooms
2 cups
baby carrots
1 small
onion, chopped
1 (14.5 ounce) can
fat-free, reduced-sodium chicken broth
1 cup
frozen peas, thawed and drained
1/2 cup
PHILADELPHIA Chive & Onion Cream Cheese Spread
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Toss chicken with flour in slow cooker
Add all remaining ingredients except peas and cream cheese spread; cover with lid
Cook on Low 6 to 8 hours (or on HIGH 3 to 4 hours), stirring in cream cheese and peas for the last 30 min
--------------------------------------------------
