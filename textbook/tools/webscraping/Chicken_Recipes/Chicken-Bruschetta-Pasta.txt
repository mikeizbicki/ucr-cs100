--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Chicken Bruschetta Pasta
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
1 pound
boneless skinless chicken breast strips
1/3 cup
fat-free reduced-sodium chicken broth
1/4 teaspoon
garlic powder
1 (10 ounce) tub
PHILADELPHIA Original Cooking Creme
2 cups
grape tomatoes
2 cups
hot cooked farfalle (bow-tie pasta)
12 leaves
fresh basil leaves, cut into strips
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Cook chicken in large nonstick skillet on medium heat 5 to 6 min. or until done. Add broth and garlic powder; cook 3 min
Add cooking creme and tomatoes; cook and stir 3 min
Stir in pasta and basil
