--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
One-Dish Chicken and Stuffing Bake
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
1 1/4 cups
boiling water
4 tablespoons
butter or margarine
4 cups
Pepperidge Farm&#174; Herb Seasoned Stuffing
6
skinless, boneless chicken breasts
Paprika
1 (10.75 ounce) can
Campbell's&#174; Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup or Campbell's&#174; Condensed 98% Fat Free Cream of Mushroom Soup
1/3 cup
milk
1 tablespoon
chopped fresh parsley
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Mix water and butter. Add stuffing. Mix lightly
Spoon stuffing along center of 3-quart shallow baking dish. Arrange chicken on each side of stuffing and sprinkle with paprika
Mix soup, milk and parsley. Pour over chicken. Cover
Bake at 400 degrees F for 30 minutes or until done
