--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Slow Cooker Spicy Buffalo Ranch Chicken
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
3 pounds
chicken breast
1 (12 ounce) bottle
hot pepper sauce (such as Frank's RedHot&#174;)
1 (1 ounce) package
dry ranch dressing mix (such as Hidden Valley Ranch&#174;)
2 (4 roll) packages
Hawaiian buns, or as needed
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Combine chicken, hot sauce, and ranch dressing mix together in a slow cooker
Cook on Medium for 6 hours. Shred chicken and serve on buns
--------------------------------------------------
