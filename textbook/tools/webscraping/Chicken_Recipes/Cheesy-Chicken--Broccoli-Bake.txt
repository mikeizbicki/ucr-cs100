--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Cheesy Chicken & Broccoli Bake
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
1 (6 ounce) package
STOVE TOP Stuffing Mix for Chicken
1 1/2 pounds
skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 (16 ounce) bag
frozen chopped broccoli, thawed
1/2 pound
VELVEETA&#174;, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1 (10.75 ounce) can
reduced-sodium condensed cream of chicken soup
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Prepare stuffing mix as directed on package; set aside
Mix chicken, broccoli, VELVEETA and soup in 13x9-inch glass baking dish; top with the stuffing
BAKE 30 min. or until chicken is cooked through
--------------------------------------------------
