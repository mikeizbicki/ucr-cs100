--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
2-Step Skillet Chicken Broccoli Divan
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
1 tablespoon
butter or margarine
1 pound
skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut up
3 cups
fresh or frozen broccoli flowerets
1 (10.75 ounce) can
Campbell's&#174; Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup (Regular or 98% Fat Free)
1/2 cup
milk
1/2 cup
shredded Cheddar cheese
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Melt butter in skillet. Add chicken and cook until browned, stirring often
Add broccoli, soup and milk. Cover and simmer 5 min. or until done. Sprinkle with cheese
