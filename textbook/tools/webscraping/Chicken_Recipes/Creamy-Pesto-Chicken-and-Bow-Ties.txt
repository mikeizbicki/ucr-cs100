--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Creamy Pesto Chicken and Bow Ties
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
3 cups
uncooked bow tie pasta
2 tablespoons
butter or margarine
1 pound
skinless, boneless chicken breast, cubed
1 (10.75 ounce) can
Campbell's&#174; Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup (Regular or 98% Fat Free)
1/2 cup
pesto sauce
1/2 cup
milk
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain
Meanwhile, heat butter in skillet. Add chicken and cook until browned, stirring often
Add soup, pesto sauce and milk. Bring to a boil. Cook over low heat 5 minutes or until done. Stir in drained pasta and heat through
