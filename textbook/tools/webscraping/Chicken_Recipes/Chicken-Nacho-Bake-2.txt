--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Chicken Nacho Bake
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
4
small boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/2 cup
chunky salsa
1/4 cup
sour cream
1/2 cup
crushed tortilla chips
1/2 cup
KRAFT Tex Mex Shredded Cheese
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Heat oven to 400 degrees F
Place chicken on foil-covered baking sheet. Slice 3 cuts on top of each chicken breast with sharp knife
Top each chicken breast evenly with salsa, sour cream and tortilla chips
Bake 20 minutes. Remove from oven; sprinkle with cheese. Bake 10 minutes or until chicken is done (170 degrees F)
