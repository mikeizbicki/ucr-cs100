--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Stir-Fry Chicken Lo Mein
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
1 (22 ounce) package
Tyson&#174; Grilled and Ready&#174; Fully Cooked Frozen Grilled Chicken Breast Strips
2 cups
sliced fresh mushrooms
2 tablespoons
vegetable oil
2 cups
frozen sweet pepper stir-fry
2/3 cup
stir-fry sauce
1 pound
linguine, prepared according to package directions
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Prepare Grilled & Ready&#174; Frozen Grilled Chicken Breast Strips according to package directions
Meanwhile, in a large skillet, cook and stir mushrooms in hot oil for 4 minutes. Add sweet pepper; cook and stir for 2 minutes. Add chicken and stir-fry sauce; cook and stir until heated through. Serve over hot cooked linguine
