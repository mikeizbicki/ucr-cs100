--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Campbell's&#174; Cheesy Chicken and Rice Casserole
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
1 (10.75 ounce) can
Campbell's&#174; Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup (regular or 98% Fat Free)
1 1/3 cups
water
3/4 cup
uncooked regular long-grain white rice
2 cups
fresh or frozen vegetables
1/2 teaspoon
onion powder
4
skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1/2 cup
shredded Cheddar cheese
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Stir the soup, water, rice, vegetables and onion powder in a 12 x 8 inch shallow baking dish
Top with chicken. Season chicken as desired. Cover
Bake at 375 degrees F for 45 minutes or until done. Top with cheese
--------------------------------------------------
