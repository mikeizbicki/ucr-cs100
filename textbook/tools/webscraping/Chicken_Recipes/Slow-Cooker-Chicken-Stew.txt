--------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------
Slow Cooker Chicken Stew
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------
3 pounds
skinless, boneless chicken breast meat - cut into bite-size pieces
1/2 teaspoon
olive oil
3 cloves
garlic, crushed
1/2 cup
water
1 (10.75 ounce) can
condensed cream of celery soup
1/2 teaspoon
ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon
salt
1/2 teaspoon
Italian-style seasoning
1
apple - peeled, cored and cut into eighths
3 small
red potatoes, cut into eighths
1
onion, cut into eighths
1 (1.25 ounce) envelope
dry onion gravy mix
--------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Saute garlic and chicken until lightly browned
Place chicken and garlic in slow cooker; add water, soup, pepper, salt, seasoning, apple, potatoes, onion and gravy mix. Mix all together
Set heat on Low setting and simmer slowly for 6 to 8 hours
